About Our Weather App

Welcome to our Weather App! Our application provides real-time weather updates for cities around the world. Whether you're planning your day or preparing for a trip, our app helps you stay informed about the weather conditions.

Our features include:

  • Current temperature
  • Weather description
  • Humidity levels
  • Wind speed
  • Weather icons for visual representation

We strive to deliver accurate and up-to-date weather information using the OpenWeatherMap API. Our user-friendly interface ensures that you can easily access the weather data you need.

Thank you for using our Weather App. We hope it helps you stay prepared and informed!
